From Ownership to Access

In the past, we bought CDs or MP3s. Now, we use the Freemium Model. You don't own the file — you pay for the right to listen whenever you want. Spotify has over 600 million users, 240 million of whom pay for Premium.

Sound Quality & Bitrate

Music streaming uses compression to save bandwidth. Standard quality is 128 kbps, High Quality is 320 kbps, and newer "Lossless" tiers (Apple Music, Tidal) offer CD quality at 1411 kbps — sounding exactly like the original recording.

The Math of Streaming

Artists are paid per stream. On average, Spotify pays between $0.003–$0.005 per stream. That means an artist needs roughly 250 streams to earn $1. A song must be streamed ~50,000 times for the artist to earn $200 — the equivalent of selling 20 CDs.

AI & Discovery

Spotify's secret weapon is its recommendation algorithm. Using machine learning, it analyzes your listening patterns to create the Discover Weekly playlist — a personalized mixtape of 30 songs you've never heard but will probably love. It has over 2 billion user streams per month.
