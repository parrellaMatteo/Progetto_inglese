Adaptive Bitrate (ABR)

Have you ever noticed quality drop for a second, then return to 4K? That's ABR in action. The player constantly monitors your internet speed and adjusts video quality on the fly — so playback never stops, even on unstable connections.

Compression & Codecs

A raw 4K movie would be hundreds of gigabytes — far too large to stream. Engines called Codecs (H.264, H.265, and the new AV1) compress the data by removing details invisible to the human eye, making it light enough to stream on a phone.

The Impact of YouTube

Every minute, more than 500 hours of video are uploaded to YouTube. This requires massive data centers and AI systems to organize, moderate, and recommend content to over 2 billion monthly users.

The Business Model

Netflix spends over $17 billion per year on original content. The strategy: exclusive shows and movies that you can't watch anywhere else. This "content moat" is why people stay subscribed — and why the streaming wars are so fierce.
