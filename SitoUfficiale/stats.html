What the Numbers Mean

The streaming industry has grown faster than almost any other sector in tech history. In just 15 years, it went from a niche experiment to accounting for the majority of all internet traffic worldwide. The global market is projected to exceed $300 billion by 2030.

Who Is Streaming?

Streaming is no longer just for young people. The fastest-growing demographic on Netflix is adults over 50. In developing countries, mobile-first streaming (on smartphones, not TVs) is growing at over 25% per year, driven by cheaper mobile data and affordable Android devices.
