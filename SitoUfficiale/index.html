Chapter 03

WHAT IS STREAMING?

The Technology Behind the Play Button

Streaming is a technology that allows us to play data instantly, creating a continuous flow of information without waiting for full downloads. From Netflix's massive bandwidth usage to Spotify's economic revolution, this technology has changed our lives forever.

Services like Cloud Gaming are the new frontier, using remote supercomputers to stream high-end games directly to your device. But we must also consider the environmental cost of the giant data centers powering this digital world.

GREEN IMPACT 🍃

Streaming feels "invisible," but it has a real physical cost. Data centers consume enormous energy for power and cooling.

📱 1 Hour Stream ~55g CO₂
🚗 1 Km Driving ~120g CO₂

Pro Tip: Watching in SD instead of 4K can reduce your carbon footprint by up to 80%!
