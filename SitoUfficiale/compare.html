Best Value Overall: Spotify

With a generous free tier, best-in-class AI recommendations, cross-device sync, and the highest user satisfaction scores, Spotify edges out Netflix as the most complete streaming service of 2024.

🎬 Netflix — Best for Binge-Watchers

Unmatched library of originals and the most sophisticated video recommendation engine. The password-sharing crackdown was controversial, but it worked: revenue grew 15% in 2024. Worth it if you watch at least 4 hours of TV per week.

🎵 Spotify — Best for Music Lovers

The free tier alone is worth having. Discover Weekly and algorithmic playlists are genuinely better than any competitor's. The transition into podcasts and audiobooks makes it a one-stop audio platform.

🎮 Twitch — Best for Gaming Community

Unique because it's live. The social interaction — chat, subscriptions, cheers — creates a community experience no other platform matches. It's not about watching; it's about being there.

🏰 Disney+ — Best for Families

Disney, Pixar, Marvel, Star Wars, and National Geographic under one roof. The cheapest way to access premium franchise content. Kids' content library is unbeatable for families.
