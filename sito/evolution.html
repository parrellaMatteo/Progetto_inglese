The Digital Revolution

From physical discs to the infinite cloud.

The Timeline of Change

Streaming didn't happen overnight. It was a war between industry giants and digital pirates.

1999

The Napster Era

The MP3 format changes everything. For the first time, music becomes "liquid" and easily shareable, even if illegally.

2005

The Birth of YouTube

Google buys a small site where users upload videos. Video streaming starts to dominate internet traffic.

2007

Netflix Goes Digital

Netflix shifts from mailing DVDs to streaming content online. The "Binge-watching" culture is born.

2008

The Spotify Solution

A legal way to access all the music in the world for a small monthly fee. Piracy starts to decline.

Today

The Cloud Everything

We don't just stream movies and music; we stream entire PC games (Cloud Gaming) and complex AI software.

Format Dominance Over Time:

CDs & Vinyl (10%)
Digital Downloads (5%)
Streaming (85%)
