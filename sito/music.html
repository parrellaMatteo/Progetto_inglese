From Ownership to Access

In the past, we bought CDs or MP3s. Now, we use the Freemium Model. You don't own the file; you pay for the right to listen to it whenever you want.

Sound Quality (Bitrate)

Music streaming uses compression to save data. Standard quality is usually 160 kbps, while High Quality goes up to 320 kbps. Some services now offer Lossless audio, which sounds exactly like the original recording.

The Math of Streaming

Artists are paid per stream. On average, it takes about 250 streams for an artist to earn 1 dollar. This has completely changed how musicians make money today.
