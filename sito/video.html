The Technology: Adaptive Bitrate

Have you ever noticed the quality drop for a second and then go back to 4K? That is ABR. The player detects your internet speed and changes the quality automatically so the video never stops buffering.

Compression & Codecs

A raw 4K movie would be too heavy for the internet. Engines called Codecs (like H.264 or AV1) compress the data by removing invisible details, making it light enough to stream on your phone.

The Impact of YouTube

Every minute, more than 500 hours of video are uploaded to YouTube. This requires massive data centers and AI to organize and recommend content to users.
