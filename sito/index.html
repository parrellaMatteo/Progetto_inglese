AI INSIGHT: THE STREAMING REVOLUTION

What are Streaming Services?

Streaming is a technology that allows us to play data instantly, creating a continuous flow of information without waiting for full downloads. From Netflix's massive bandwidth usage to Spotify's economic revolution, this technology has changed our lives forever.

As explained in our AI presentation, services like Cloud Gaming are the new frontier, using remote supercomputers to stream high-end games directly to your device. However, we must also consider the environmental cost of the giant data centers that power this digital world.

HOW IT WORKS?

Streaming uses CDNs and Buffers to deliver data faster.

GREEN IMPACT 🍃

Streaming feels "invisible," but it has a physical cost. Data centers use massive amounts of energy for power and cooling.

📱 1 Hour Stream ~55g CO2
🚗 1 Km Driving ~120g CO2

Pro Tip: Watching in Standard Definition (SD) instead of 4K can reduce your carbon footprint by up to 80%!
